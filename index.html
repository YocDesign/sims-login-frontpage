  • Q2. How can this SIS help me better manage my school?
    1. The campus director can identify trends and concerns quickly, and is able to address them immediately.
    2. The school managers work more closely together in order to improve all areas of school operations.
    3. Provides Every School report including fees, students enquiry, student enrolments, student performance evaluation and more.
  • Q3. We are looking for a complete school management system which can be customized according to our school process.?

    Yoctel SIS is designed in such a way that any customization or addition /removal of modules are easy. New custom fields can be added in few minutes.

    Yes. SIS generates reports for all your centers on single platform. We have customers with the same scenario.

  • Q6. How secure is my data?
